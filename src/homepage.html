About Us

Ainz Oool Gown was created by our family and our friends Ti, Fuyu, and Tsuki all of whom we met while playing Blade & Soul. At the moment we are a small group but this makes up the founders of our awesome clan! Even though the spelling is slightly off our clan name comes from Overlord, one of the greatest anime’s EVER.

We have always loved gaming, but the gaming community is not always the most considerate or helpful (especially in MMO’s). Finally having enough of the “noob, idiot and wtf’s” we would see, directed towards others or even ourselves (Because all these people were never new and learning right). We decided to create our own guild where people would’nt have to deal with these comments or the rude people in pick up groups (PUGs).
